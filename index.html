Cover Letter

Hans McMurdy graduated from Northern Arizona University in 2013 with a degree in Applied Sociology. In senior year he started exploring entrepreneurship via startup weekend. After several teams fell apart (as they normally do after startup weekend) he started to learn how to code through Codecademy, Udemy and many other online platforms in september of 2013. In that time he's explored a number of areas. Starting with the fundementals of front-end web development including responsive design for both web site and email templetes (now expanding to Sass). On his spare time he also enjoys hacking hardware with arduino and experimenting with wearable technologies and Internet of Things using a combination of communications from IR, Bluetooth, and wifi.

As an Account executive at LilliMedia he gained expeiance with b2b relations and front-end development. Additionally he has worked on several campaigns as both an organizer and as a developer proving him with experiance in project management, social media and email marketing campaigns. Currently he works as a freelance developer for small businesses, non-profits, political organizations and is actively engaged in the entrepreneur community.
